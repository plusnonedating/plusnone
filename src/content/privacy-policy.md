# Plus None Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: May 29, 2026*
*Last Updated: May 29, 2026*

This Privacy Policy describes how **Plus None LLC** ("Plus None," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information about you when you use Plus None — including plusnone.fetewell.com, our QR codes and signage at partner venues, our @plusnonedating Instagram and TikTok accounts, and any related services.

This policy is separate from the privacy policy of Fêtewell venues (operated by Dear Properties LLC at fetewell.com/privacy-policy), even though Plus None currently lives on a subdomain of fetewell.com.

## 1. Information We Collect

### When you submit a profile (consumer users)

Information you give us directly:
- Name (first name, or first + last)
- Age
- Gender and who you're interested in
- Email address
- A 10-30 second selfie video
- A one-line "ask me about..." prompt
- Instagram or TikTok handle (only if you opt in to social posting)

Information collected automatically:
- A randomly generated device identifier (stored locally in your browser, see Section 5)
- Your IP address
- Browser type, device type, operating system
- The time of your submission
- Your approximate location at the moment you submit (used once to confirm you're at the partner venue, then discarded as raw GPS data)

### When you become a Partner (businesses)

Information you give us directly:
- Business name and legal entity name
- Contact name, email, phone number
- Billing address (collected by Stripe)
- Shipping address for signage
- The address of the venue you want geo-tagged
- For pop-ups: event name, dates, branding files (logo, brand colors)
- Payment information (collected and stored by Stripe — we don't see or store full card numbers)

### When you visit our website or socials

- IP address, device info, pages viewed (basic web analytics)
- Engagement signals from Instagram or TikTok if you interact with @plusnonedating (likes, comments, follows) — handled per those platforms' policies

## 2. How We Use Your Information

**For consumer users:**
- Show your profile to other singles in the same Plus None partner venue's dating pool (if you opted in to the live pool)
- Post your profile to @plusnonedating on Instagram and TikTok (only if you opted in)
- Confirm via geolocation that you're physically at a partner venue
- Power the 6-hour rolling visibility window
- Anonymized analytics for partner reports
- Customer support if you email us
- Legal and safety: investigate abuse, take down content, comply with law

**For partners:**
- Provision your geo-gating and ship signage
- Bill your subscription or one-time event fee
- Deliver your monthly or post-event report
- Promote your venue on @plusnonedating
- Account management and support

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We share information in the following limited ways:

- **With other Plus None users in your venue's dating pool** — your submitted profile (name, age, video, ice-breaker) is visible to other patrons who scanned at the same venue, during the 6-hour window.
- **On our social media accounts** (@plusnonedating, @TheVenueCEO) — if you opted in, your video and handle may be posted publicly.
- **With partner venues, in aggregated form only** — partners receive anonymized scan and demographic data about who came through their venue. **Partners never see individual user profiles, names, or contact info.**
- **With service providers** — Stripe (payments), Vercel (hosting), WordPress and Airtable (content and CRM), Zapier (workflow), Google Workspace (email). These vendors process data for us under their own privacy commitments.
- **For legal reasons** — if required by law, subpoena, or to investigate violations of our Terms of Service.
- **In a business transfer** — if Plus None LLC is sold, merged, or restructured, your information may transfer as part of that transaction.

**We do not sell your personal information.** We do not use your information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

## 4. Geolocation Data

We request your device location only at the moment you submit a profile, to confirm you're physically at a Plus None partner venue.

- We don't track your location after you submit.
- We don't sell or share location data.
- We don't store the raw GPS coordinates after the venue check — we record only that you passed the check at a given venue.

You can deny location access; in that case, the pool feature won't work, but you can still opt in to be posted on @plusnonedating.

## 5. Device Identifier (Browser Storage)

When you first visit plusnone.fetewell.com, we generate a random identifier (a "device ID") and store it locally in your browser. This ID is not tied to your name, email, or any personal information by itself.

We use it to:
- Recognize repeat visits from the same browser
- Produce anonymized, aggregate analytics for partner venues (e.g., "X% of singles who scanned at this venue came back within 90 days")

What the device ID is **not**:
- Not shared with venues at the individual level — only as part of aggregated totals
- Not used for advertising
- Not a third-party tracker — first-party only
- Not synced across devices or browsers

The device ID persists in your browser until you clear your local storage for plusnone.fetewell.com.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and similar technologies, all first-party:
- The device ID described above (localStorage, not a traditional cookie)
- Standard session and authentication tokens for partners using their Stripe customer portal

We do not currently use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or other third-party advertising trackers on plusnone.fetewell.com. (Note: the parent fetewell.com domain may use those — see their privacy policy.)

We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Submitted profiles (consumer users)** may be retained indefinitely for archival, editorial, and promotional purposes, even after public visibility expires. The 6-hour window applies only to public visibility on the dating pool page — not to our internal records or backups.
- **Partner records** are retained for as long as your account is active, plus 7 years for tax and financial recordkeeping.
- **Payment records** are retained by Stripe under Stripe's policies.
- **Device IDs** are retained until you clear your browser storage.

You can ask us to delete your submitted profile at any time — see Section 9.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Plus None is for adults 18 and over. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we learn we've collected information from a minor, we'll delete it. If you believe a minor has submitted to Plus None, email **plusnone@fetewell.com** immediately.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights including:
- **Access** — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
- **Deletion** — ask us to delete your submitted profile and associated records
- **Correction** — ask us to fix inaccurate information
- **Opt-out** — withdraw consent to social media posting at any time (we'll remove the post within 7 days)

To exercise any of these rights, email **plusnone@fetewell.com** with the subject "Privacy Request" and tell us which right you're exercising. We'll respond within 30 days.

### California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

You have specific rights including: right to know what we collect, right to delete, right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" (we don't do either), and right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

Categories of personal information we collect: identifiers, audio/visual information (your submitted video), commercial information (partner billing), internet activity (device ID, pages viewed), geolocation (one-time at submission), and inferences from any of the above.

We don't sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

To exercise CCPA rights, contact us at **plusnone@fetewell.com**.

## 10. Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit, access controls, and trusted hosting and payment providers. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If a breach affects your information, we'll notify you and any regulators as required by law.

## 11. International Users

Plus None operates from Maryland, USA. If you're using Plus None from outside the US, your information will be transferred to and processed in the US. By using Plus None, you consent to that transfer.

## 12. Third-Party Links and Embedded Content

Plus None may link to or embed content from third-party services (Instagram, TikTok, payment processors). We're not responsible for the privacy practices of those services — review their policies separately.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the current version. We'll notify active partners by email of material changes at least 14 days before they take effect. For consumer users, the updated policy is effective when posted.
